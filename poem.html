MY DEVIL

Aishwarya


It began on a beautiful winter’s twilight:

began on a beautiful winter’s twilight:

I was the laziest student around,

She was the most boring devil.


She was my friend

My boring friend,

My devil.


We used to skip so well together,

Back then

We wanted to eat together, around the world.

We wanted it all.


But one twilight, one beautiful twilight,

We decided to eat too much

Together we ruined the adventure

It was the end of our venture.


From that moment our relationship changed

She grew so pointless

And then it happened

Oh no!


She spoiled all my fun

Alas, fun!

My friend spoiled the fun

I wished I could run.


But still, she is in my thoughts

I think about how it all changed that twilight

That beautiful winter’s twilight
